This was my very first Python project, it can run from your terminal. It runs by selecting a word randomly from the list that i made, counting the amount of letters and replacing them with this '_'. One you guess a correct letter it replaces '_' with the letter that you just found. You get 5 wrong guesses till you lose.

I added a limited amout of possible words but you can change that very easily by adding strings with your desired words to the list.
